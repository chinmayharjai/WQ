Fill your bank account details

AS34454
  • 14 days ago
  • Updated
  • ~2 minute read
Not yet followed by anyone

When your background check is complete, you will receive notification by email and in the Brain Portal to enter your bank account details in Workday.

Candidates have to update their Bank account detail only AFTER they receive an email requesting to update bank account details on workday. If you try to update your bank account details before completion of background verifications, you will see an error message.

  1. Please sign in to Workday through your Brain Account


  2. In Workday, click on “Contact and Banking” icon
  3. Then click on “Change Settlement Bank Accounts”
  4. You will see the banking details table as below.
    - If you shared your banking details with us previously, it will be shown here. You will still need to confirm the information or update the information if it has changed. Click “OK” on the bottom to proceed.
    - If the bank account section is blank, Click “OK” on the bottom to input your bank info.


  5. Click ‘Attachments' and add a corroborating document which could bank statement, check or a screenshot of bank details.
  6. To update or review your bank account information, please update/review the fields marked with a red box in the following country-specific guideline*, and make sure they are filled.
    * Each country has different requirements, kindly check the guideline for your country below.
  7. Click “Submit” at the bottom of the screen after you have updated or reviewed your information. Then “Done” to exit the banking module.

Please  note that if you get an error, please cancel out of it by using the following steps:

  1. Go back into the Contact and Banking Worklet and access the View > Settlement Bank Accounts
  2. You should see a pending change at the top, where if you click the related actions, you can cancel it
  3. Go back to the Contact and Banking Worklet and Change Settlement Bank Accounts again. You can input and submit the details again.

Congratulations! You have completed all steps to become a WQ Brain Research Consultant!

Your status in WQ Brain will change in the next few minutes, and you will be able to be paid as Consultant.
